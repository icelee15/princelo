About Us



Princelo Studio was established in 2019 by a partner of innovative creatives united by a deep love for capturing life's treasured moments. Our goal is to deliver outstanding photography and videography that enables individuals and businesses to narrate their distinct stories, highlight emotions, and create lasting impressions.


Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: to freeze time and preserve memories for generations to come. We hold that every moment, regardless of its significance, deserves to be captured and treasured.


Visit us at Ednalizamon Resort, Purok 5, Angeles, Pampanga. Our state-of-the-art studio features top-notch equipment and amenities, designed to provide a comfortable and inspiring experience for our clients.
